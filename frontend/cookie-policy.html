Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or tablet when you browse our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide statistical information about how users interact with our services.

2. Cookies We Use

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

Essential/Security Cookies

  • csrftoken - Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks (Django security)
  • sessionid - Manages your login session and authentication
  • access_token_jwt - Stores your JWT access token for API authentication
  • refresh_token_jwt - Allows session refresh without re-login

Purpose: These cookies are essential for website security, user authentication, and preventing unauthorized access.

Functional/Preference Cookies

Local Storage Keys:

  • feroxTimes_currentUser - Stores your user profile information for faster loading
  • feroxTimes_bookmarks - Remembers articles you've saved for later reading
  • push_prompt_dismissed - Tracks if you've dismissed notification prompts
  • waitingForEmailVerification - Temporarily stores email during verification process

Purpose: These cookies enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences and saved content.

Analytical Cookies

  • article_viewed_* - Tracks which articles you've already read to avoid re-showing them

Purpose: Helps us understand user behavior and improve content recommendations.

3. Duration of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser (CSRF, Session tokens)
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a specified period to enhance your experience (JWT tokens, preferences)

4. Managing Cookies

You have the right to control cookies stored on your device:

  • Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being set
  • You can delete stored cookies at any time through your browser settings
  • For local storage: Use browser developer tools to clear stored preferences

Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using certain features of our website.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Currently, Ferox Times does not use third-party cookies from external analytics services. All cookies are first-party and directly managed by our website.

6. Security & Privacy

All cookies are transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. We do not store sensitive personal information (passwords, payment details) in cookies. For more information about how we handle your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
