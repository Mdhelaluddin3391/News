About Forex Times

Real-time market intelligence for global investors and readers.

Forex Times is a global financial news platform that provides in-depth coverage of currencies, commodities, stocks, and macroeconomic events. Our mission is to empower professionals, traders, and enthusiasts with accurate, up-to-date stories, expert analysis, and actionable insights.

Founded in 2026, Forex Times has grown into a reputable source for market-moving headlines and thoughtful commentary from our team of experienced economists, market analysts, and reporters.
